Hi!

I am a PhD student in the Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I am currently working in the Intelligent and Inclusive Interaction (Triple-Eye) Group and am a member of the Center for Accessibility and Inclusion Research (CAIR). My research interests are in the area of human-computer interaction (HCI) and assistive technologies. Specifically, I am working on projects related to AI-assisted user experience (UX) analysis methods and visual analytic tools to support UX practitioners.


I'm looking forward to solving real-world problems and making an impact! If you would like to talk about my work or get connected, don't hesistate to send me an e-mail or connect via LinkedIn.
