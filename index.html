Bio

I am a PhD student in the Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at Rochester Institute of Technology and a member of the Center for Accessibility and Inclusion Research (CAIR). I am honored to be a recipient of the 2023 Google PhD Fellowship in Human-Computer Interaction!


At the forefront of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and User Experience (UX), my research focuses on designing and evaluating human‑AI collaborative tools for improved usability and UX analysis. My work not only advances technology but also champions accessibility, focusing on inclusive computing education and assistive technology for diverse user groups. This website unfolds my journey of blending research with a commitment to inclusive, user‑centered technology development.


If you would like to talk about my work or get connected, don't hesistate to send me an e-mail or connect via LinkedIn.
